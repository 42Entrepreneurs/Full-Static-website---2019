What is Hook?

Hook is a very selective program designed for founders during the most difficult time, to help them start their company without worries. The first batch is happening from the 4th of June to the 3rd of September.

It is inspired by YCombinator which helped startups like Dropbox, Airbnb or Stripe to launch.

For whom?

Hook is built for the most ambitious entrepreneurs from 42. There is no other condition.
